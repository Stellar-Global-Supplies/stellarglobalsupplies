# Overview

## Project Name
Stellar Global Supplies — Ops Control Center

## One-Liner
Internal operational control center and multi-agent AI workforce dashboard for B2B steel supplier operations management.

## Problem It Solves
Stellar Global Supplies, a B2B supplier of Stainless Steel and Mild Steel products based in Pune, India, needs centralized visibility into sales, purchases, inventory, and customer operations. Manual tracking across spreadsheets and disconnected systems creates data silos, delays in decision-making, and inconsistent customer communication. The operations team lacks real-time insights into revenue, margins, and inventory levels.

## Solution
A Progressive Web App (PWA) that consolidates operational data into a single dashboard with seven specialized AI agents powered by AWS Bedrock. The system ingests CSV/JSON sales and purchase data into Supabase for analytics, while maintaining live business metrics in DynamoDB. AI agents provide grounded insights using real business data for sales forecasting, demand planning, customer communication, and operational analysis. The serverless AWS architecture ensures scalability and zero infrastructure maintenance.

## Who Uses It
Operations managers and executives at Stellar Global Supplies, a B2B steel supplier serving enterprise and mid-market customers in Pune, India. The primary users are the CEO and operations team who need real-time visibility into sales performance, inventory levels, purchase orders, and customer quotations.
